ABOUT OUR COLLEGE


I.B.(PG) is a premier co-educational government-aided institution situated on GT Road in Panipat,Haryana.affilated with kurukshetra university, it offers diverse undergraduate and postgraduate programs across arts,science,and management.

HISTORY OF COLLEGE

Founded in 1956,I.B.(PG)College in panipat is a premier,government-aided co-educational institution.Initially established as a founded to empower women,it transitioned into a co-educational institute in 1966 and has grown into a major regional center for higher education.
